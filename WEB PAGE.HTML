About the School

School Motto: Education is Light

Slogan: Making a Difference

Values: Integrity, Faith, Truth, Love, Prayer, Trust in God, Truth, and Service

Background: St Joseph's Hill SS is a Catholic founded school sponsored by priests and brothers of the Congregation of Holy Cross, operating in Kyarusozi Catholic Parish, Fort Portal Diocese. The school was established in 1996 and is fully registered by the Ministry of Education and Sports. The school is both day and boarding.

Location: The school is located 23km off Fort-Portal-Kampala highway from Rugombe Town Council. It is situated in Kyembogo Sub-county in Kyenjojo District.

School Vision: Educate the youth to be useful citizens in society.

School Mission: To provide a well-balanced education and promote quality job makers and leaders.
